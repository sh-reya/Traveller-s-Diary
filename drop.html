Tipu's Drop



One of the best things about this place is that it can be visited at any time of the year. All you've got to do is pick a date and pack your bags.Located on a hilltop on a cliff which is 600 metres above the elevated ground, Tipu's Drop is one of the oldest and most famous tourist attractions in Nandi. While this place is known for being the cliff from where the prisoners were pushed off, it also offers a spellbinding view of the town. It is the best Nandi Hills view point for gazing at splendid views of the town below.
