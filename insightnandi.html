Nandi Hills (Anglicised forms include Nandidurg and Nandydoorg) is an ancient hill fortress built by Ganga Dynasty and later enlarged and strengthened by Tipu Sultan in southern India, in the Chikkaballapur district of Karnataka state. It is 10 km from Chickballapur town and approximately 60 km from the city of Bengaluru. The hills are near the town of Nandi. In traditional belief, the hills are the origin of the Arkavathy river, Ponnaiyar River, Palar River, Papagni and Penna River. Watching the sunrise at Nandi Hills is popular with tourists. The first ever SAARC summit hosted by India was held at Nandi Hills in 1986.


The nearest railway station is at Chikkaballapur (9km), and the nearest airport at Bangalore (58km), from where taxis are available to Nandi Hills. Buses are also available from Bangalore and most neighbouring cities. You could also hire a cab or self-drive as well.


1.AIR
The nearest airport is in Bangalore. You could take a bus from there to Nandi Hills.
Nearest Airport: Kempegowda International Airport (BLR)


2.TRAIN
The nearest railway station is Chikkaballapur, from where you could drive, take a cab or a bus to Nandi Hills.


3.ROAD
Tourists and visitors can drive down to this hillock which is 60 km from the city centre. There are cabs operating to Nandi Hills as well. Parking Visitors would have to park their individual vehicles at the designated place at the foothills. Further on, take a bus or any other transport provided by the Nandi authorities to reach the hilltop. This arrangement would avoid traffic on the road uphill.
Autorickshaws as well as taxis can be opted for by tourists to travel within the city. Most sites are best explored on foot.





1. Camping
One of the best things to do in Nandi hill to experience natural beauty closely is camping. One shall indulge in camping and stay overnight under the stars. The location of the camps in foothills allows taking multiple adventures like rope activities, ziplining etc. and delve in the magic of town which is surrounded by lush green peaks.


2.Trek To Skandagiri Hills
Skandgiri hills, also known as Kalavara Durga, is one of the most classic trekking spot. Trekking to it is one of the best things to do in Nandi Hills as it makes one feel refreshed with view of dense clouds, vegetation and nature closely. Pack the bags this vacation and head to the trekking spot. With a view of rare birds, dense clouds and sun playing hide & seek, head to the top while crossing variety of trails. Visit old rocky temple at the top and capture best of the shots by your camera and eyes!


3.Paragliding in Nandi Hills
Imagine yourself flying high and descending with passion and thrill! This feeling is welcomed at Nandi Hills and one can soak in the beauty of wonderful location from the high sky. With utmost safety and proper instructions, one will be taken to the peak from where the adventure will begin. You will be jumping the cliff and come down with the help of open parachute tied behind your back. Make some wonderful videos and get captured with huge smiles.


4.Visit to Tipu's Drop
A hilltop cliff at a height of 600m attracts thousands of visitors to get a soothing view from the top. Nature lovers can enjoy a sense of peace away from the city life. With a chilling history of punishing people while dropping them from the cliff in Tipu Sultan’s era, one can now enjoy the view and click thousands of pictures from the top. Enjoy the peace and tranquility and sit back while basking in sun and winds. And yes, for the safety and precautions, enough of the arrangements have been already made. So, be carefree and chill at the Tipu’s drop. The place is indeed one of the best things to do in Nandi hills and not to be missed by any traveler.





1.TIME
Its best to reach Nandi Hills by 5:30am and wait for the sunrise and then carry on towards the temple (on the top of the hill) and the surrounding gardens (which boasts of a wide variety of flora). You can also plan to visit during sunset.


2.CLOTHING
Wear comfortable walking shoes or flats. High heels, etc would ruin your otherwise pleasant romantic experience.


3.SEASON
Usually, In summer (March-May) temperatures are from 23 to 40-degree Celsius. Best time to visit Nandi hills by 05:30 AM, we can have a sunrise view. In monsoon (June - August) though Nandi hills receive the moderate rain falls during this duration.
