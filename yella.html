Punganoor Lake Park, Nature Park, Jalagamparai Waterfalls, Telescope Observatory, Jalagandeeswarar Temple, Swamimalai Hills, Velavan Temple, Government Herbal Farm, Yelagiri Adventure Camp and other amazing places. Soothe your senses with the spectacular Yelagiri tourist spots, which offer the visitors an unparalleled scenic beauty along with unending fun and frolic. The parks and farms such as Fundera and Silk farm regale the young and the old and imprint unforgettable memories. While balmy weather and tranquillity are the striking features of the places to visit in Yelagiri, its misty mountains, wonderful landscapes and several sightseeing places like Nilavoor Lake, and Amirthi waterfalls leave nature lovers lost in the sublime beauty of the places. The rose and orchid gardens of Yelagiri present a scene to behold and etch an impregnable memory on the visitors' mind. Also, the topography of the hill station makes it a favourite destination for a host of natural sports like paragliding, rock climbing, trekking, boating, and what not.

So, get ready to camp all night under the stars and enjoy the bonfire with your loved ones ;-)
